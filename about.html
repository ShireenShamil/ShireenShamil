ABOUT US

Panadura Undergraduate Association (PUA) is a non-profit organization dedicated
to empowering and guiding the youth of Panadura and its surrounding areas.

Comprised of passionate and driven undergraduate students, our mission is to
support the educational and personal development of local students.

We are engaged with O/L, A/L & Undergraduates students and organize seminars,
motivation programmes, leadership programmes and guidance etc.

VISION

❯

"To be the cornerstone of student life in the Panadura area,fostering a community where
undergraduates thrive acaddemically, socially and professionally."

"Our mission is to provide a platform for Panadura area Undergraduates to connect , grow
and succeed through advocacy, support services, and collaborative initiatives that enrich
their educational journey and contribute positively to the local community"
